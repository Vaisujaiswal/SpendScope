# Economics

## What Is A Converted Lead Worth To Credex?

Credex makes money by helping companies buy discounted AI infrastructure credits.

Example:

If a startup spends:
- $2,000/month on AI tools

and Credex helps them save:
- 20–30%

then Credex could potentially earn:
- a referral fee
- margin on credits
- infrastructure resale revenue

Assuming Credex earns around:
- $1,500–$3,000 total value per converted customer annually

a strong qualified lead could realistically be worth:
- $150–$400

depending on company size and monthly AI spending.

High-spend engineering teams are significantly more valuable because they continue purchasing credits over time.

---

# Why This Could Work

The product has strong viral potential because:
- audit results are shareable
- founders already discuss AI costs publicly
- the tool gives immediate value before asking for email signup
- infrastructure savings are easy to understand financially

Unlike many SaaS products, the value proposition is very direct:
- “save money on AI tooling.”

That makes the product easier to distribute organically compared to more abstract productivity software.

---

# Biggest Risk

The biggest challenge would be maintaining:
- accurate pricing data
- trustworthy recommendations
- high-quality consultation conversion rates

If recommendations feel inaccurate or overly sales-focused, trust and conversion rates would drop quickly.

The product succeeds only if users believe the audit is genuinely useful before any sales conversation happens.
